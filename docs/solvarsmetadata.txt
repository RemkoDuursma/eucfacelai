Solar position and day length data estimated for the EucFACE location. Generated with getRichmondSolar from the HIEv R package. Data in 30min timestep until 2020. 

Variables: 
  sunrise: time of sunrise (hours) 
  sunset: time of sunset (hours) 
  daylength: daylength (hours) 
  sunelevation: solar elevation (degrees from horizon) 
  DateTime: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM
